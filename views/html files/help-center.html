Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get support

How do I create an account?

Click on "Create Account" from the login page, fill in your details including your name, email, password, and select at least one favorite genre. Once submitted, you'll be redirected to the login page.

How do I add movies to my watchlist?

Browse movies on the Movies page or from your Dashboard. Click on any movie card to view details, then use the "Add to Watchlist" button to add it to your list. You can organize movies into "To Watch", "Watching", or "Completed" categories.

Account & Profile

How do I update my profile?

Go to your Profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can update your information, favorite genres, and other preferences from there.

How do I change my password?

Currently, password changes can be requested through our support team. Contact us at support@watchly.com for assistance.

Movies & Reviews

How do I write a review?

Navigate to any movie's detail page, scroll to the "Write a Review" section, select your rating (1-10), write your review, and click "Submit Review". Your review will be visible to other users.

Can I edit or delete my reviews?

Yes, you can edit or delete your own reviews from the movie detail page. Look for the edit and delete icons next to your review.

Community Features

How do I join discussions?

Visit the Discussions page to see all active conversations. Click on any discussion to read and participate. You can also create your own discussion post by clicking "Create Post".

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends page and browse suggested friends or search for users. Click "Send Request" to send a friend request. Once accepted, you'll be able to see each other's activity and recommendations.

What are Events?

Events are watch parties organized by community members. You can join existing events or create your own. Events allow you to watch movies together with friends and discuss them in real-time.
